# 📅 Monthly Plan

High-level monthly objectives mapped to milestones (~6 months total, ~20 hrs/week).

| Month | Window | Milestones | Primary outcomes |
|:-----:|--------|:----------:|------------------|
| 1 | Jun–Jul 2026 | M0 → M1 (start M2) | Ecosystem live; LLM fluency; first project (`structured-extractor`). |
| 2 | Jul–Aug 2026 | M2 | Production RAG with eval harness (`rag-knowledge-assistant`). |
| 3 | Aug–Sep 2026 | M3 | Agents + MCP (`agentic-workbench`). |
| 4 | Sep–Oct 2026 | M4 | Evals, observability, guardrails (`llm-eval-kit`); retrofit M2/M3. |
| 5 | Oct–Nov 2026 | M5 | Cloud deploys + CI/CD; light fine-tuning (`lora-finetune-lab`). |
| 6 | Nov–Dec 2026 | M6 | Capstone (`flagship-ai-platform`); system design; interview-ready. |

> Dates are targets, not deadlines — the approval gate after each milestone is the real pace-setter.

## Monthly review questions

- Did the milestone hit its completion criteria?
- Which scorecard areas moved? Which are lagging?
- Is the portfolio visibly stronger (new repo, better READMEs, live demo)?
- What should next month do differently?
